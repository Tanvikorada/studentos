# StudentOS

> The Operating System for Scholars

StudentOS is an AI-native academic workspace designed to replace the dozens of tabs students keep open every day.

Instead of switching between task managers, GPA calculators, AI tools, resume builders, interview platforms, notes, and productivity apps, StudentOS brings everything into one focused environment.

---

## Why StudentOS?

Most students work across multiple disconnected tools:

- Notes in one app
- Tasks in another
- AI chat in a browser tab
- Resume builder elsewhere
- GPA tracking in spreadsheets
- Mock interviews on separate platforms

StudentOS combines these workflows into a single productivity system designed specifically for students.

## Development Journey

StudentOS started as a broad platform with 19 different modules.

Through user-focused iteration and hackathon development, the platform was simplified into a focused set of workflows that support how students actually work.

The goal is not to build more features.

The goal is to help a student open StudentOS in the morning and organize their entire academic day without leaving the platform.
